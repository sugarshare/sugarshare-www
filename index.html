Welcome to SugarShare! Beta

Create a shareable link valid for 24 hours to transfer files privately and securely 🚀!

Why SugarShare 🚀 ?

Sharing files should be simple — as simple as uploading files to a secure location and sending it over to anybody you want.

With SugarShare you can upload your files to a secure and temporary location in the cloud, and get a link in return which you can share with anybody. The files stay in their location for 24 hours before expiring, and can only be accessed via the provided link — i.e. by you and your collaborators.

How does it work 😃 ?

  1. Install the Chrome extension
  2. Select the file to share
  3. Copy the generated link and share it with your collaborators and friends!

FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions 🙋

How long are the files stored?
24 hours before getting deleted.
What is the maximum file size?
SugarShare is in Beta mode currently, and is limited to 10 MB. But don't worry! a much larger cap will be available soon 🤠.
Who can access the uploaded files?
Nobody except who you share the link with — i.e. you and your collaborators.
